✈️ Trips

Tokyo

I want to walk through the bright streets of Tokyo with you, hand in hand, discovering a world so different yet beautiful — just like us. I imagine late-night walks under glowing lights, laughing together, tasting new food, and creating memories we’ll talk about for the rest of our lives.

Switzerland

I dream of Switzerland with you — calm mornings, fresh mountain air, peaceful lakes, and moments of silence that feel full instead of empty because you’re there. Just us, nature, love, and the kind of peace that only comes when you’re with the right person.

Paris

Paris with you feels like a dream. Walking along the streets, sharing kisses, watching sunsets, and falling in love all over again. Paris is known as the city of love, but even there, my love for you would be the most beautiful thing of all.

Barcelona

Barcelona with you would be full of life — warm nights, music, laughter, beaches, and endless smiles. I imagine dancing, exploring, and enjoying every moment together, feeling free and completely happy just by being next to you.

🏡 Dreams

My biggest dream in life is simple: you being happy. I want to give you all the happiness you deserve — every single day. I want to support you, protect you, and stand by your side through everything life brings.

I dream of waking up knowing I can make you smile, knowing that I’m the person you trust, the person you love, the person you feel safe with. I want to build a life that feels warm, loving, and full — a life where you always feel cherished and appreciated.

💍 Life Together

I want to marry you. I want to spend my entire life choosing you, over and over again. I want every morning, every evening, every quiet moment and every loud laugh to be shared with you.

I want to build a home with you — a peaceful, loving place filled with warmth and laughter. I dream of having two children with you, one daughter and one son, and raising them in a home full of love, respect, and happiness.

Growing old with you isn’t just something I want — it’s something I see. You are not just a part of my future; you are my future.
