✈️ Travel

I want to travel to Turkey with you — exploring beautiful places, eating amazing food, and losing ourselves in the culture together. I imagine sunsets, long walks, spontaneous adventures, and the kind of memories that stay with us forever because they were made together.

🍜 Food

I want to try everything with you — laughing over food, sharing bites, and discovering new favorites together. From pizza in Italy, sushi in Japan, croissants in Paris, street food from different cultures, to cozy homemade meals — food tastes better when it’s shared with you. Every meal with you would feel like a date, no matter where we are.

🎬 Movies

I want to watch every kind of movie with you — scary ones where we hold each other closer, funny ones where we laugh until our stomachs hurt, and romantic ones that make us fall in love all over again. Movies like The Conjuring, It, The Notebook, About Time, or Crazy Rich Asians — but honestly, the movie doesn’t matter. What matters is sitting next to you, sharing those moments together.
